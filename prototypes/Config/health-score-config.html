How Health Score is Calculated

The Knowledge Center health score is a composite metric that evaluates the quality, usability, and technical soundness of your knowledge articles. It is calculated across three main dimensions, each with configurable weightages that roll up into an overall score from 0–100.

1
Scans & Articles
Evaluates individual article quality through automated scanning — checking for duplicates, user engagement signals, SEO optimizations, and AQI scoring.
2
Knowledge Base
Assesses the overall health at the knowledge base level — covering content quality, usage activity, search effectiveness, and article freshness.
3
System Environment
Measures system-level indicators — instance uptime, response performance, configuration compliance, and active integration health.
